Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade my plan?

Click the "Upgrade Now" button on your desired plan. You'll be directed to our secure payment page where you can complete your purchase.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your premium features will remain active until the end of your billing period.

What happens when my subscription expires?

When your subscription expires, your account will automatically revert to the free plan. You'll need to renew your subscription to regain premium features. Lifetime subscriptions never expire.

What's the difference between monthly, yearly, and lifetime plans?

Monthly plans are billed every month at ₦2,500. Yearly plans are billed annually at ₦25,000 (saving you ₦5,000 compared to monthly). Lifetime plans are a one-time payment of ₦60,000 with no recurring charges and never expire.

How do I qualify for the Content Creator plan?

To qualify, you need at least 10,000 followers on a social media platform and a promotional video about BINK with at least 20,000 views. Click "Apply Now" to submit your application.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile money payments. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
