Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a BINK account?

Creating a BINK account is easy! Here's how:

  1. Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Choose a unique username (this will be used for your bio link: bink.com/u/username)
  4. Create a password (minimum 6 characters)
  5. Confirm your password
  6. Click "Sign Up" to create your account

Once your account is created, you can start with a free plan and upgrade to premium features anytime.

How do tokens work?

Tokens are BINK's virtual currency that allow free users to access premium templates without a subscription:

  • You can purchase 800 tokens for ₦500
  • First-time token purchasers receive 200 bonus tokens
  • Premium templates cost between 100-200 tokens each
  • You can earn free tokens by completing tasks
  • Premium users get access to all templates without using tokens
  • Once you purchase a template with tokens, it's yours to keep forever

Your token balance is displayed in your dashboard sidebar and can be clicked to purchase more tokens.

Can I change my template after creating my bio page?

Yes! You can change your template at any time without losing your content or changing your bio link URL:

  • Your bio link URL remains the same, so you don't need to update it on your social profiles
  • All your content (links, profile information, etc.) automatically transfers to the new template
  • Free users can use free templates (Classic and Glassmorphism) or purchase premium templates with tokens
  • Premium users can switch between any template at any time without additional cost
  • Templates you've purchased with tokens remain available to you even if you downgrade from a premium plan

To change your template, go to the bio editor page and select a different template from the template selector.

What's the difference between free and premium plans?

Here's a comparison of our plans:

Free Plan (₦0):

  • Up to 3 links
  • Basic analytics
  • Free templates (Classic and Glassmorphism)
  • BINK branding on your bio page
  • Option to buy premium templates with tokens
  • Single bio link

Premium Plan:

  • Monthly: ₦2,500/month
  • Yearly: ₦25,000/year (Save 17%)
  • Lifetime: ₦60,000 one-time payment
  • Unlimited links
  • Advanced analytics with location data
  • Access to all premium templates
  • No BINK branding
  • Priority support
  • Custom domain (coming soon)

Free users can also access premium templates by purchasing them individually with tokens.

How do I qualify for the Content Creator plan?

The Content Creator plan gives you premium features free for 3 months. To qualify, you need to meet at least one of these requirements:

  • Have at least 10,000 followers on a social media platform
  • Have at least 20,000 video views on your content

Benefits of the Content Creator plan include:

  • All Premium features included
  • Verified creator badge on your profile
  • Featured placement in the creator directory
  • Monetization options (coming soon)

To apply, select the "Content Creator" option on our pricing page. Our team will review your application and verify your follower count or video views before approving your account.
