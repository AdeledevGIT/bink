How Tokens Work

For Free Users: Tokens allow you to use premium templates without subscribing to a premium plan:

  • Each premium template costs a specific number of tokens to unlock
  • Once you use tokens on a template, you can use that template indefinitely
  • Tokens expire after their validity period
  • You can purchase different token packages based on your needs

For Premium/Creator Users: You already have unlimited access to all templates without needing to purchase tokens!

Consider upgrading to a Premium or Creator plan for unlimited access to all templates and additional features!
