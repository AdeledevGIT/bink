Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is BINK?

BINK is a bio link platform that lets you create a customizable page with all your important links in one place. It's perfect for social media profiles where you can only have one link in your bio.

How do I get started?

Sign up for a free account, choose your username, and start adding links. You can customize your page with different templates, add a profile picture, and write a bio to personalize your page.

What templates are available?

We offer 20+ professionally designed templates across multiple categories: Minimalist (Classic, Glassmorphism), Neon & Cyberpunk (Neon Card, Tech Wave), Corporate, Creative Artist, Magazine Style, and more. Free users get 2 templates, while Premium users access all templates.

What is the Media & Catalog feature?

Premium users can add YouTube videos, image galleries, music from all platforms, and create product catalogs with buy buttons. This turns your bio page into a complete multimedia showcase and online store.

How does the token system work?

Tokens let free users buy premium templates without a subscription. Templates cost 100-200 tokens each. You can buy 800 tokens for ₦500, with 200 bonus tokens on your first purchase. Premium users get all templates included.

What analytics do you provide?

Free users get basic analytics (views, clicks, click rates). Premium users get advanced analytics including visitor locations, device types, referrers, time-based insights, and detailed performance metrics for all content types including media and catalog items.

Can I change my template later?

Yes! You can change your template anytime. Your bio link stays the same, so you don't need to update it on your social profiles. All your content automatically transfers to the new template.
