Introduction to BINK

BINK is a comprehensive bio link platform designed to help creators, influencers, and businesses share all their important links in one place. With BINK, you can create a customized page that showcases your content, social media profiles, products, and more.

Our platform offers a range of features to help you create a professional and engaging bio page:

Link Management

Add, edit, and organize your links with ease. Track clicks and engagement.

Beautiful Templates

Choose from a variety of free and premium templates to customize your page.

Detailed Analytics

Track visitor behavior, geographic locations, and engagement metrics.

Token System

Purchase tokens to unlock premium templates without a subscription.

Getting Started

Creating Your Account

To get started with BINK, you'll need to create an account:

  1. Visit the Sign Up page
  2. Enter your email address and create a password
  3. Choose a unique username for your BINK page
  4. Complete your profile by adding a profile picture and bio

Dashboard Overview

After signing in, you'll be taken to your dashboard. Here you can:

  • View your page statistics (views, clicks, engagement)
  • Access your bio page editor
  • Manage your links
  • View and purchase tokens
  • Access analytics
  • Update your profile settings

Your BINK URL

Once you've created your account, you'll get a unique BINK URL in the format:

bio.html?u=yourusername

This is the link you'll share on your social media profiles and other platforms. When someone visits this link, they'll see your customized bio page with all your links.

Adding Your First Links

To add links to your bio page:

  1. Go to the "Edit Bio" page from your dashboard
  2. Click the "Add New Link" button
  3. Enter a title and URL for your link
  4. Add an optional description
  5. Select a platform icon (optional)
  6. Click "Save" to add the link to your page

Note: Free users can add up to 3 links to their bio page. To add more links, you'll need to upgrade to a Premium plan.

Bio Editor

The Bio Editor is where you customize your bio page, add links, and choose templates.

Profile Information

At the top of the Bio Editor, you can update your profile information:

  • Profile Picture: Upload a profile picture by clicking on the profile image circle
  • Display Name: Set the name that will appear on your bio page
  • Bio Text: Write a short description about yourself or your brand

Managing Links

The Links section allows you to add, edit, and organize your links:

  • Add Link: Click the "Add New Link" button to create a new link
  • Edit Link: Click the edit icon on any existing link to modify it
  • Delete Link: Click the delete icon to remove a link
  • Reorder Links: Drag and drop links to change their order on your page

Preview Your Page

The right side of the Bio Editor shows a live preview of your bio page. You can:

  • See Changes in Real-time: Any changes you make will be reflected in the preview
  • Open in New Tab: Click "Open in New Tab" to view your page in a full browser window
  • Copy Link: Click "Copy Link" to copy your bio page URL to share with others

Tip: Make sure to click "Save" after making changes to ensure they're applied to your live bio page.

Templates

BINK offers a variety of templates to customize the look and feel of your bio page.

Free Templates

All users have access to these templates at no cost:

  • Classic: A clean, simple design with a focus on your links
  • Glassmorphism: A modern, translucent design with a frosted glass effect

Premium Templates

These templates are available to Premium subscribers or can be purchased with tokens:

  • Purple Card: A vibrant purple design with card-style links
  • Neon Card: A bold neon design with glowing elements
  • Black Landing: A sleek black template with centered about section
  • Tech Wave: A futuristic tech-themed template with wave animations
  • And more... New templates are added regularly

Selecting a Template

To choose a template for your bio page:

  1. Go to the Bio Editor page
  2. Scroll down to the "Choose a Template" section
  3. Click on a template to preview it
  4. Click "Use This Template" to apply it to your bio page

Note: If you're a free user and select a premium template, you'll be prompted to either purchase it with tokens or upgrade to a Premium plan.

Token System

BINK's token system allows free users to access premium templates without subscribing to a Premium plan.

How Tokens Work

Tokens are a virtual currency within BINK that can be used to purchase premium templates:

  • Each premium template costs between 100-200 tokens
  • Once you purchase a template with tokens, it's yours to use forever
  • Premium and Creator plan subscribers have access to all templates without using tokens

Purchasing Tokens

To buy tokens:

  1. Go to the Tokens page from your dashboard
  2. Select a token package (800 tokens for ₦500)
  3. Click "Buy Tokens" to proceed to payment
  4. Complete the payment process
  5. Tokens will be added to your account immediately after payment verification

First Purchase Bonus

New users receive 200 bonus tokens on their first token purchase. This bonus is automatically added to your account after your first purchase is verified.

Tip: If you plan to use multiple premium templates, it may be more cost-effective to upgrade to a Premium plan, which gives you access to all templates.

Analytics

BINK provides detailed analytics to help you understand your audience and track the performance of your bio page.

Basic Analytics (Free Plan)

Free users have access to essential analytics data:

  • Total Views: The number of times your bio page has been viewed
  • Total Clicks: The number of times visitors have clicked on your links
  • Click Rate: The percentage of views that result in clicks
  • Link Performance: See which links are getting the most clicks

Advanced Analytics (Premium Plan)

Premium users get access to comprehensive analytics features:

  • Visitor Demographics: Information about your visitors' devices, browsers, and referrers
  • Geographic Data: See where your visitors are coming from with location tracking
  • Time Analysis: Track when your page gets the most traffic
  • Performance Over Time: View trends and patterns in your page's performance
  • Detailed Reports: Get insights into visitor behavior and engagement

Accessing Analytics

To view your analytics:

  1. Go to the Analytics page from your dashboard
  2. Free users will see the Basic Analytics tab by default
  3. Premium users will see both Basic and Advanced tabs
  4. Use the filters to customize the data view (time range, chart type, etc.)

Note: Analytics data is updated in real-time, so you can always see the most current information about your bio page's performance.

Pricing & Plans

BINK offers different plans to suit your needs and budget.

Free Plan

The Free plan is perfect for getting started with BINK:

  • Up to 3 links on your bio page
  • Basic analytics
  • Access to free templates
  • Ability to purchase premium templates with tokens
  • 200 free tokens on first purchase

Premium Plan

The Premium plan offers enhanced features for serious users:

  • Unlimited links
  • Advanced analytics with location data
  • Access to all premium templates
  • No need to purchase tokens
  • No BINK branding on your page
  • Priority support

Pricing:

  • Monthly: ₦2,500 per month
  • Yearly: ₦25,000 per year (Save 17%)
  • Lifetime: ₦60,000 one-time payment

Content Creator Plan

The Content Creator plan is designed for influencers and content creators:

  • All Premium plan features
  • Verified creator badge on your profile
  • Featured placement in the creator directory
  • Upcoming monetization options

Requirements:

  • At least 10,000 followers on a social platform
  • At least 20,000 video views
  • Application approval required

Pricing: ₦6,000 for 3 months

Token Packages

For users who want to access premium templates without a subscription:

  • 800 tokens for ₦500
  • Premium templates cost 100-200 tokens each
  • 200 bonus tokens on first purchase

Payment Options

BINK offers several payment methods to make it easy for you to upgrade your plan or purchase tokens.

Available Payment Methods

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Pay securely with your card
  • Mobile Money: Use mobile payment services
  • Bank Transfer: Make a direct bank transfer (requires manual verification)

Payment Process

To make a payment:

  1. Select your desired plan or token package
  2. Click the "Buy" or "Upgrade" button
  3. You'll be redirected to the payment page
  4. Select your preferred payment method
  5. Complete the payment process
  6. For bank transfers, upload your payment receipt for verification

Payment Security

All payments on BINK are secure. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your payment information, and we never store your full card details on our servers.

Note: After making a payment, you'll receive a confirmation email with your receipt. If you don't receive this email, please check your spam folder or contact our support team.

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is BINK?

BINK is a bio link platform that allows you to create a customizable page with all your important links in one place. It's perfect for social media profiles, especially platforms that only allow one link in bio.

How do I get started with BINK?

Simply sign up for a free account, choose your unique username, and start adding links to your page. You can customize your page design, add a profile picture, and write a bio to make it truly yours.

Templates & Customization

What templates are available?

BINK offers a variety of templates to make your bio page stand out. Free templates include Classic and Glassmorphism. Premium templates include Purple Card, Neon Card, Black Landing, Tech Wave, and more.

Can I customize my bio page?

Yes! You can customize your bio page by adding a profile picture, writing a bio, and choosing a template that matches your style. Premium users have access to more customization options.

Tokens & Pricing

How does the token system work?

Tokens allow free users to purchase premium templates without subscribing to a premium plan. Each premium template costs between 100-200 tokens. You can buy 800 tokens for ₦500, and you'll receive 200 bonus tokens on your first purchase.

What happens to my premium templates if I cancel my subscription?

If you cancel your Premium subscription, your account will revert to the Free plan at the end of your billing period. However, any premium templates you've purchased with tokens will remain available to you.

Technical Support

How do I get help if I have a problem?

If you need assistance, you can contact our support team by emailing support@bink.com. Premium users receive priority support.

Is my data secure on BINK?

Yes, we take data security seriously. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
